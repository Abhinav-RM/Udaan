📜 Manual of Spells 📜
🖐️ SELECTION
Open both hands (palms out) to enter Selection Mode. Hover over parts to highlight them in a glowing blue coating.
🤏 CONFIRM
Pinch both hands simultaneously to lock the selection.
✋ MANIPULATE
Once confirmed, drop one hand. Move the remaining hand to drag the part away.
🔄 ROTATE
Move the hand in a circular motion to spin the selected part.
💥 BREAK
While a part is selected, use one hand to perform a fast "chopping" motion across the object to split it in half.
